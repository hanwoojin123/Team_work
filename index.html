SMOOTH

Traditional liquor made using the "cheungcheungjiju" technique, which requires a lot of care, is sweet and soft to match the name "JIJU" so you can enjoy it without any burden.

DELICATE

You can feel the subtlety and luxuriousness of traditional alcohol through the process of making the delicate liquor of "JIJU" which deviates from the stereotype of Takju's clunkyness.
